About Us.

At SAMAS, we specialize in providing unforgettable villa experiences in the heart of Bali. With a focus on the vibrant and sought-after Canggu area, our portfolio includes 7 prime locations tailored to suit your needs.

Whether you’re planning a short getaway or a long-term stay, we offer flexible rental options, daily, weekly, monthly, and yearly — to match your needs.

Each of our villas is thoughtfully designed to offer comfort, style, and convenience in Bali’s most iconic hotspot areas.

Our Services

Laundry Service

Enjoy the convenience of free pick-up and delivery right from your room. Only the laundry itself is charged — perfect for a hassle-free, fresh wardrobe during your stay.

Airport Pick-Up Service

Start your stay stress-free with our reliable airport pick-up service. Our driver will greet you at the terminal and ensure a smooth transfer to your accommodation.

Room Cleaning

We provide daily room cleaning to keep your space spotless and comfortable throughout your stay.

Floating Breakfast

Elevate your morning with a beautiful floating breakfast experience in the pool. Available with an additional fee.

24-Hour Customer Service

Our team is available 24/7 to assist you with anything you need— from bookings to last-minute requests.

Celebration Services

Make your special moments (birthday, anniversary, etc) unforgettable with our balloon and surprise setup. Celebration services available for an additional fee.
